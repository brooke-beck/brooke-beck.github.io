Flint, our first cat

This is Flint, our first cat. I also got him while still living with my parents. He had neurological issues and seizures, but he was the most gentle cat you'd ever meet. He passed away in January of 2024. We still miss him almost two years later.

Roadblock, "Blocc"

This is Roadblock ("Blocc"), another one of our cats. We brought him home in May of 2023 so that Flint would have a buddy. We are not sure of his exact age, just that he is large and very vocal. Blocc's favorite things are treats and attention.

March 22nd, 2025

This is Gub Gub. We found Gub Gub as a stray in one of our sheds in June of 2023. We cleaned her up, took her to the vet, and she became ours. We noticed she was not growing much at all. By the time our vet figured out that she had FIP, it was too late to try and get treatment. Gub passed in the spring of 2024, and like Flint, we still miss her.

Torpedo

One of our more recent additions, Torpedo! We brought Torpedo home around May of this year. She was part of a catch-and-release program to reduce the stray population, but the program administrators deemed her too friendly to be released. Now she lives with us! She is a chaotic girl, and she looks a little funny from having teeth removed - but we adore her.
